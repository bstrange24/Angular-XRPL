Check Constraints
Description
Authorized Account Only
Only the Destination account (set in CheckCreate) can cash the check.
Check Must Exist
The check must still be in the ledger and not already cashed, expired, or canceled.
Sender Must Have Funds
The sender (check creator) must have sufficient XRP or IOU balance to cover the amount at the time of CheckCash.
Specify Amount OR DeliverMin
You must provide either Amount (exact cash amount) or DeliverMin (minimum acceptable), not both.
No Partial XRP Checks
XRP checks must specify exact Amount. DeliverMin is only allowed for IOU currencies.
Trust Line Required for IOUs
The recipient must have a trust line to the issuer (if the check is for an issued currency like USD, RLUSD, etc.).
Respect SendMax Limit
The cashed Amount or DeliverMin must be less than or equal to the SendMax value from the original CheckCreate.
Check Can Expire
If Expiration was set in CheckCreate, the check cannot be cashed after that time (based on XRPL close time).
